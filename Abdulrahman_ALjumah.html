About This Website

This website helps you learn four key English skills: Reading, Writing, Grammar, and Speaking. You will find a weekly syllabus, daily lessons, and an interactive quiz to test your knowledge. Start learning in a fun and easy way!

📅

Syllabus

📌 Week 1: Reading & Vocabulary
Short texts and understanding main ideas
📌 Week 2: Writing Basics
Writing simple sentences and paragraphs
📌 Week 3: Grammar (Present Simple)
Affirmative, negative and questions
📌 Week 4: Speaking & Pronunciation
Introduction and daily conversations

📚 Today's Lesson

Reading Text:
"I wake up at 7 am. I eat breakfast and drink coffee. Then I go to school. I study English and Math. I come back home at 3 pm."

New Vocabulary: Wake up, eat breakfast, go to school, come back home.
Grammar Rule: Add (s) to the verb with He/She/It (e.g., He wakes up).
